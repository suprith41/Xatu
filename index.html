The Art of Deception

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Hider secretly selects a briefcase and hides the object inside it
  2. Hider gives one optional clue - make it misleading or helpful, your choice
  3. Guesser picks a briefcase. Guess right and you win instantly
  4. Guess wrong and the briefcase with the object is removed. Roles swap and the game continues

Last mind standing wins.
